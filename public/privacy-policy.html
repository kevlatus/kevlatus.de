Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how kevlatus Peak collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use the app.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information from you: Google Calendar: Your Google Calendar events, including the event name, date, time, location, and attendees. Fitbit: Your Fitbit data, including your activities, sleep patterns, and heart rate.

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use your personal information to provide you with the features and functionality of kevlatus Peak. For example, we use your Google Calendar events to help you sync your availability to other calendars shared with friends or family without exposing your personal event details to them. We use your Fitbit data to help you track your progress and to provide you with personalized recommendations.

How We Share Your Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties without your consent. We may share your personal information with third parties if we are required to do so by law or if we need to do so to protect our rights or the rights of others.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your personal information: You can choose to allow or deny access to your Google Calendar events and Fitbit data. You can choose to delete your account and all of your personal information at any time.

How We Protect Your Personal Information

We use a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and access controls.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated privacy policy on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at peak@kevlatus.de.
